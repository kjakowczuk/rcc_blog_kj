Introduction

Questions

1.) Which region has the most hours spent on social media platforms?

2.) What’s the most used social media platform and what’s the social media addiction score?

Predictions

I predict a positive relationship occurring in which students who live in the United States spend the most hours on Instagram with a social media addiction score falling in between the 8-10 range, with a median score of 8.

Data Source

  • Students’ Social Media Addiction: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/adilshamim8/social-media-addiction-vs-relationships/data

Methods

Data Source

  • What is the source?

    • Source: Students’ Social Media Addiction

  • How was the data collected?

    • This data was collected in a one-time online survey administered in the first quarter of the year. Participants are then recruited through their university mailing lists and social-media platforms, ensuring diverse results. This data was collected in 2025.

  • What are the variables included?

Key Variables
Variable Description
Student_ID Unique respondent identifier
Age Age in years
Gender “Male” or “Female”
Academic_Level High School / Undergraduate / Graduate
Country Country of residence
Avg_Daily_Usage_Hours Average hours per day on social media
Most_Used_Platform Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, etc.
Affects_Academic_Performance Self-reported impact on academics (Yes/No)
Sleep_Hours_Per_Night Average nightly sleep hours
Mental_Health_Score Self-rated mental health (1 = poor to 10 = excellent)
Relationship_Status Single / In Relationship / Complicated
Conflicts_Over_Social_Media Number of relationship conflicts due to social media
Addicted_Score Social Media Addiction Score (1 = low to 10 = high)

  • How many records/rows/participants?

    • There are 13 variables with 705 participants, totaling to 706 rows.

  • Any other relevant information

    • In this project, country was changed to region to match the various types of countries listed for ease, but left alone as a variable.

    • Duplicate entries were removed due to the unique Student_ID participants entered in the survey.

    • This survey was completely anonymous.

Question 2 - What’s the most used social media platform and what’s the social media addiction score?

Graph 1

  • Code:

ggplot(
  data = students_social_media_addiction,
  mapping = aes(y = Most_Used_Platform))+ geom_bar(fill = "blue", alpha = 0.5) + labs(
    title = "Most Used Social Media Platform"
  )
#ggsave(filename = "most_used_platform.png")
  • In conclusion, Instagram is the most used social media platform.

  • In conclusion, Instagram’s social media addiction score is between 5-8, with the highest median score of 7.

Discussion

  • Overall, my hypothesis is supported and therefore I believe it more. The reason being is that I claimed students living in the United States, so in the Americas region, spend the most hours on social media platforms. Initially, I had Tiktok as my predicted most used platform, but I changed it to Instagram instead since I believe students, like myself, use that platform the most. At first, I predicted that students would fall in the 8-10 range for the social media addiction score on Instagram. This prediction isn’t completely wrong, however the score range I predicted was too high and predicted median was one higher than the resulted median.
